# What is SaskPoly?

**SaskPoly** (short for **Saskatchewan Polytechnic**) is Saskatchewan's primary public institution for post-secondary technical education and skills training. It is recognized nationally and internationally for its expertise, innovation, and focus on applied, hands-on learning.

---

## Quick Facts

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Full Name** | Saskatchewan Polytechnic |
| **Formerly Known As** | Saskatchewan Institute of Applied Science and Technology (SIAST) |
| **Type** | Public polytechnic institution |
| **Founded** | 1959 (as Saskatchewan Technical Institute) |
| **Rebranded** | September 24, 2014 (became Saskatchewan Polytechnic) |
| **Students** | ~26,000 distinct students annually |
| **Campuses** | 4 physical campuses + extensive distance education |
| **Member of** | Polytechnics Canada (since 2012) |

## Campuses

Saskatchewan Polytechnic operates four main campuses across the province:

1. **Moose Jaw Campus** (Palliser Campus)  
   600 Saskatchewan Street & 6th Avenue NW  
   Located in the fourth-largest city in Saskatchewan, known for its historic downtown and underground tunnels.

2. **Prince Albert Campus** (Woodland Campus)  
   Located in the third-largest city in Saskatchewan, serving northern communities.

3. **Regina Campus** (Wascana Campus)  
   4500 Wascana Parkway  
   Located in Saskatchewan's capital city, one of Canada's fastest-growing major cities.

4. **Saskatoon Campus** (Kelsey Campus)  
   Multiple locations including 1130 Idylwyld Dr N  
   Located in the largest city in Saskatchewan, the province's economic and cultural hub.

## What is a Polytechnic?

A polytechnic is a type of post-secondary institution that focuses on **applied learning** — combining theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on experience. Key characteristics include:

- **Industry-driven curriculum** developed in partnership with employers
- **Hands-on training** in labs, workshops, and real-world work placements
- **Multiple credential levels**: certificates, diplomas, degrees, and apprenticeship training
- **Applied research** that supports innovation and solves real industry problems
- **Strong graduate employment rates** due to direct alignment with labour market needs

Saskatchewan Polytechnic is the **first and only polytechnic** in the province of Saskatchewan.

SaskPoly offers education across every economic and public service sector, including:

- Hospitality & Tourism
- Creative Arts & Design

### Credential Types

| Credential | Duration | Description |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| **Certificate** | 1 year or less | Focused training for entry-level positions |
| **Diploma** | 2 years | Comprehensive technical training |
| **Degree** | 4 years | Applied bachelor's degrees (e.g., Psychiatric Nursing) |
| **Apprenticeship** | Varies | Combine on-the-job training with classroom instruction |
| **Continuing Education** | Flexible | Short courses and professional development |

## Why It Matters

Saskatchewan Polytechnic plays a critical role in:
